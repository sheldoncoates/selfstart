What I believe

I believe movement is life.
I believe you can heal your body with functional movement patterns.
I believe it is never to late to create change in a body & it can happen fast.
I believe your body knows what it needs.
I believe every system in your body relies on a functional musculoskeletal system.

All systems in the body work on movement:
  • the heart beats
  • the lungs breath
  • the stomach grinds
  • the colon transports
  • the blood flows
  • the nerves conduct
  • the muscles contract
Dis-ease in the body begins when movement is impaired. Restore your proper design for movement and your pain will cease. No fancy machinery or magic pills required; your body already has everything it needs to heal.

I believe you need to trust yourself.
I believe you need to take responsibility for your present state of health.
I believe you can become free of your pain.

Who am I?

Stephanie Marcotte is a registered physiotherapist. She is a graduate of the University of Toronto Physical Medicine program. Stephanie has more than 25 years of clinical experience in musculoskeletal health.

Stephanie has completed numerous continuing education courses in advance manual therapy, movement and exercise therapies. She has an integrative approach to assessment and treatment. She believes in helping people help themselves.

Stephanie has designed her practice so that she assesses and treats clients individually. Her clients are not delegated to support staff. Her approach is very dynamic and she has been a source of inspiration for many clients over the years.
